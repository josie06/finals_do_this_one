Need some help? This is how the code works:

To add code:

- Click the "Add Item" button on the home page.

- Open the Navigation bar at the top of your screen, then select "Add New Item".

To remove items in your list:

- Click on the item you would like to delete

- Click "Yes" to remove it from your list

- If you would like to return to your list without making any changes, click "No"

To go to the original python code:

- Open the navigation bar at the top of your screen, then select "Go To Original Code!!"
